Delicious Grinder Sandwich!

This italian inspired sandwich is sure to make you smile!

This sandwich was reportedly created in the 1950's in Philadelphia by Italians working in Shipyards known as Hog Island.
It's name supposedly was a nod to the tough italian bread used to make the sandwich, which you would have to grind your your teeth through.
These days, the grinder is making its rounds on popular entertainment app, Tiktok!
The grinder can be made with pretty much whatever you want, but some of the staples include:

  • Sub roll, french bread or equivalent hoagie
  • Italian meats like salami and pepperoni and ham
  • Tangy iceberg lettuce slaw
  • Fresh cut tomatoes
  • Your choice of cheese! I like provolone and muenster.
  • Pepper rings
  • Salt, pepper, oregano
  1. Prepare your slaw!
      2. Shred head of iceberg lettuce
      Add mayonnaise, red wine vinegar, spices and peppers and mix well
  2. Preheat oven to 425
  3. Cut sub roll in half, line with cheese and meats
  4. Place in oven until cheese is melted and bread is toasted
  5. Top with sliced tomatoes and tangy slaw
  6. Consume quickly, it's gonna be messy!
